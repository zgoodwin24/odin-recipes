Quesadilla

Description

pretty good easy meal

Ingredients

  • 2 Tortillas
  • Mexican cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. heat butter in pan
  2. lay tortilla in the pan
  3. put a healthy amount of cheese on the tortilla
  4. put anothre tortilla on top of the cheese
  5. When cheese has melted, flip quesadilla
  6. When both sides are cooked, plate and serve
